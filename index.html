Privacy Policy for Z Venue

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Z Venue ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Z Venue. This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application and its associated services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless venue booking experience, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, phone number, and email address (collected during sign-up via Firebase).
  • User-Generated Content: Profile pictures and photos of venues that you choose to upload.
  • Location Data: Approximate and precise location to help you find venues near you.
  • Transaction Data: Details regarding the venues you book. We do not store your credit card or bank details; these are handled securely by Razorpay.

2. Device Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Camera: To allow you to take and upload photos of venues or update your profile picture.
  • Location: To provide geographic-based venue searches and proximity features.
  • Storage: To access and upload photos from your device's gallery.

3. Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Z Venue platform.
  • Process your bookings and facilitate communication with venue owners.
  • Improve and personalize your app experience.
  • Send you transaction notifications and updates.

4. Third-Party Services

We share data with trusted third-party providers only as necessary to provide our services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database management, and app performance monitoring.
  • Razorpay: For processing secure payments. Please refer to Razorpay's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account is active.

Account Deletion: You can request to delete your account and all associated data at any time via the settings in the app or by emailing us at zwiftypt25@gmail.com. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including Firebase's secure infrastructure, to protect your data. However, no electronic transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Z Venue does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13.
